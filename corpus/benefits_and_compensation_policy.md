# Benefits and Compensation Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Policy Owner:** Human Resources & Total Rewards  
**Applies To:** All full-time employees (part-time per separate addendum)

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines eligibility, enrollment, and administration of employee benefits and compensation programs. It ensures consistent, equitable, and compliant administration of health, retirement, and supplemental benefits alongside base and variable pay structures.

---

## 2. Compensation Philosophy

The company targets the **50th–75th percentile** of market median for base salary by role, level, and geography. Compensation decisions consider role scope, performance, tenure, internal equity, and budget availability. Pay equity reviews are conducted annually by HR and Finance.

### 2.1 Pay Bands

| Level | Grade Range | Review Cycle |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| Individual Contributor | IC1–IC6 | Annual |
| Manager | M1–M4 | Annual |
| Director+ | D1–D3 | Annual + mid-year market adjustment |

Merit increases are typically effective April 1 following annual performance reviews. Promotional increases may occur at any time with VP approval.

### 2.2 Variable Compensation

Eligible roles may receive annual bonuses tied to company and individual performance. Commission plans apply to designated sales roles per Sales Compensation Plan SCP-100. Bonus targets are documented in offer letters and the HRIS.

---

## 3. Health and Welfare Benefits

### 3.1 Eligibility

Full-time employees working 30+ hours per week are eligible on the **first day of the month following hire date**. Domestic partners and dependents may be enrolled per plan rules.

### 3.2 Medical, Dental, and Vision

The company offers PPO and HDHP medical plans. Employee cost share is 20% of premium for employee-only coverage; dependent coverage subsidized at 50%. Open enrollment occurs each November for January 1 effective dates. Qualifying life events permit mid-year changes within 30 days.

### 3.3 Life and Disability

Basic life insurance (1x salary) and AD&D provided at no cost. Supplemental life available via payroll deduction. Short-term disability (STD) and long-term disability (LTD) replace 60% of base salary after waiting periods defined in plan documents.

### 3.4 Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

Confidential counseling and referral services available 24/7 at no cost to employees and household members. Contact **1-800-555-0142** or **eap.company.com**.

---

## 4. Retirement Benefits

### 4.1 401(k) Plan

Eligible employees may contribute pre-tax or Roth deferrals up to IRS limits. Company match: **100% of first 3%** and **50% of next 2%** of eligible compensation, vested immediately. Enrollment available upon eligibility via Fidelity portal.

### 4.2 Financial Wellness

Quarterly webinars and one-on-one consultations available through the retirement plan provider at no cost.

---

## 5. Time-Based and Supplemental Benefits

- **Paid holidays:** 10 per calendar year
- **PTO:** Per PTO and Leave Policy
- **Parental leave:** Per PTO and Leave Policy
- **Tuition reimbursement:** Up to $5,250/year with 24-month service obligation
- **Wellness stipend:** $50/month for gym, fitness apps, or wellness programs

---

## 6. Enrollment and Changes

**Step-by-step enrollment (new hires):**

1. Review benefits guide within 5 days of hire
2. Attend new-hire benefits orientation (scheduled by HR)
3. Make elections in Workday within **30 days** of eligibility date
4. Receive confirmation email and ID cards within 10 business days
5. Contact **benefits@company.com** for questions

Failure to enroll within 30 days defaults to benefits waived until next open enrollment except for auto-enrolled benefits (basic life, EAP).

---

## 7. Compensation Adjustments and Appeals

Employees may request compensation review discussions during annual review or upon significant role change. Appeals of merit decisions must be submitted to HR within **15 business days** of notification with supporting documentation. HR schedules calibration review with manager and skip-level within 10 business days.

---

## 8. Compliance

Benefits administration complies with ERISA, ACA, COBRA, and applicable state laws. Summary Plan Descriptions (SPDs) are the controlling documents in case of conflict with this policy summary.
